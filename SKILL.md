\# SKILL.md - License Recommendation Skill



\## Skill Overview



This skill is designed to intelligently recommend open-source licenses (such as MIT, GPL, Apache, etc.) to users. The user may or may not describe their project and their preferred method of open-sourcing. This skill aims to interact with the user in the most down-to-earth and easy-to-understand manner to understand their needs and recommend the most suitable open-source license. It emphasizes efficiency in questioning, aiming to gather all necessary information in as few interactions as possible, avoiding overly abstract questions.



\## Functionality Details



\### User Interaction Example



\*\*User\*\*: I am a programmer. I have written an AI agent software that helps people better retrieve their documents. I want to make it open source and need to adopt an open-source license. Which open-source license should I use?

\## Implementation Notes



\- \*\*Efficiency\*\*: Aim to minimize the number of interactions needed to gather enough information to make a recommendation.

\- \*\*Clarity\*\*: Avoid asking overly abstract questions; instead, focus on practical aspects relevant to the user's project.

\- \*\*Personalization\*\*: Tailor recommendations based on the specifics of the user's project and their stated preferences.



By referencing existing practices and cases, this skill will assist users in selecting the most appropriate open-source license for their projects, ensuring they meet both their goals and legal requirements efficiently.
